Setting up a new Stripe account can take as little as a few minutes.
Requirements You must operate in a supported country (https://stripe.com/global). If you do not, contact your Onboarding Specialist for further guidance.
Why this step is necessary - To integrate with VRBO or Expedia.
- To integrate with Booking.com, unless you're using "Payments by Booking.com".
- To enable online payments for your website.
